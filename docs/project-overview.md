# Project Overview

## Problem

Chinese Gaokao application planning is a high-stakes family decision. Students and parents must compare schools, majors, regional constraints, score position, policy rules, and future study or career preferences in a short decision window.

The difficulty is not only the amount of information. The harder problem is that families need to turn incomplete, emotional, and sometimes conflicting preferences into a plan that is understandable, adjustable, and reviewable.

## Target Users

The intended users are:

- Students preparing Gaokao application choices.
- Parents or family members involved in the decision.
- Human advisors who need structured information before consultation.
- Early-stage education consulting operators who need a repeatable advisory workflow.

The current repository does not include real user profiles or customer cases.

## Why AI Is Useful Here

AI is useful when it is placed inside a controlled workflow. In this project, AI can help:

- Convert unstructured family input into a structured planning profile.
- Identify missing or conflicting preferences.
- Generate clarification questions before recommendations are drafted.
- Summarize public or advisor-approved information.
- Produce readable explanations of trade-offs and risk categories.
- Draft a report for human advisor review.

The project is not built around the idea that AI should decide the final application plan. Its value is in information structuring, explanation, and workflow support.

## Why Human Review Is Still Necessary

Human review is required because Gaokao planning involves uncertainty, changing public information, personal values, and family-specific constraints. A model can help organize reasoning, but it cannot fully understand the emotional, financial, regional, and long-term consequences of a student's decision.

Human advisors are needed to:

- Check whether the profile reflects the student's real situation.
- Validate whether retrieved information is current and relevant.
- Review risk explanations for overconfidence.
- Discuss trade-offs with the family.
- Decide whether the final summary is suitable for use in consultation.

## Portfolio Boundary

This repository is a portfolio-safe case study. It intentionally excludes sensitive materials and focuses on the design logic behind the system.

It does not contain:

- Real student or parent information.
- Chat records or private consultation notes.
- API keys, tokens, credentials, or `.env` files.
- Private prompts or internal ranking formulas.
- Commercial pricing or internal business documents.
- Claims that the system is fully production-ready.

All examples in this repository are fictional and synthetic.
